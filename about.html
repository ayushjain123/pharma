ABOUT THE PROJECT

This project is made after understanding the problems faced by people who get confused in purchasing the pharamceutical items due to various online/offline services offered by vendors and charge extra money for it.
In India, there is one more important point is thatr people don't trust services easily. They want to compare services with each other before using them.
Hence this platform will provide them all the necessary information about online services, offline vendors related to pharamceutical sector.
This platform will also give the information about the doctors also.
